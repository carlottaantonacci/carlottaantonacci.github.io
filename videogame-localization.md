<h1> Graphical Elements </h1>

<p> Video game localization often also requires modifying graphic elements such as journals or maps that convey the lore behind the story itself. In the case of NITW, Mae's journal is an element that adds an extra layer of sarcasm and dark humor, and it was necessary to translate and adapt it to provide a complete experience. </p>
